Alice Phoebe Lou

An introduction

Born in South Africa and currently residing in Berlin, Alice Phoebe Lou is an independent singer-songwriter known for her ethereal voice, raw lyricism, and fierce artistic integrity. Blending elements of folk, jazz, indie rock, and soul, she has crafted a distinctive sound that resonates with vulnerability and defiance in equal measure.

Alice began her musical journey busking on the streets of Berlin, where her spellbinding live performances quickly garnered attention. Choosing to remain independent from major labels, she has self-released multiple albums and EPs, including Orbit (2016), Paper Castles (2019), Glow (2021), and Shelter (2023). Her songs often explore themes of love, identity, femininity, and the emotional complexities of modern life.

Celebrated for her poetic songwriting and unfiltered expression, Alice has cultivated a devoted global following without compromising her vision. Whether performing on a street corner or a concert stage, she brings an intimacy and authenticity that makes each listener feel personally seen.

This trio of polaroids showcases Alice Phoebe Lou’s three most recent albums, each paired with a favourite track that speaks to the soul of the record.
